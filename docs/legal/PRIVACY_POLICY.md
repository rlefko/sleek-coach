## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Email address
- Password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing)

**Profile Information**
- Display name
- Height

**Health and Fitness Data**

- Coaching session history

**Device Information**
- Device type and operating system
- App version
- Unique device identifiers (for authentication)

**Usage Data**
- Features used and interaction patterns
- Session duration and frequency
- Error logs and crash reports (anonymized)

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

- **Encryption in Transit**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS encryption
- **Encryption at Rest**: Data stored in our databases and file storage is encrypted
- **Secure Password Storage**: Passwords are hashed using Argon2id (industry-leading algorithm)
- **Access Controls**: Employee access to user data is strictly limited and logged
- **Secure Photo Storage**: Progress photos are stored with encryption and accessed only via time-limited signed URLs

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Specific retention periods:

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account & Profile | While account is active |
| Check-ins & Nutrition | While account is active |
| Progress Photos | While account is active |

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

### Right to Access
You can export a copy of all your personal data at any time through the app's Settings > Account > Export Data feature.

### Right to Deletion
You can delete your account and all associated data through the app's Settings > Account > Delete Account feature. After a 30-day grace period, all data is permanently deleted.

### Right to Correction
You can update your profile information at any time through the app.

### Right to Portability
Your exported data is provided in a standard JSON format that can be used with other services.

### Right to Withdraw Consent
You can withdraw consent for optional data processing (such as AI web search features or analytics) at any time through Privacy Settings.

### Right to Object
You may object to certain types of data processing by contacting us.

### GDPR Rights (European Users)
If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

### CCPA Rights (California Residents)
If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (note: we do not sell personal information).
